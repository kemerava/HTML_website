Here is why we like these animals

List of fun rabbit facts
  1. Rabbits are very cute and sweet.
  2. Rabbits are very social creatures that live in groups.
    • The European rabbit lives underground, in burrows.
    • A group of burrows is known as a warren.
  3. Rabbits’ eyes are on the sides of their head, meaning they can see almost all the way around them.
  4. A rabbit’s teeth never stop growing!
  5. Like cats, happy rabbits purr when they’re content and relaxed.
  6. Rabbits are amazing athletes — they can jump as high as 90 centimetres in one leap!
  7. A rabbit’s best feature? Their long ears! Growing up to 10 centimetres in length, rabbits can turn their ears by 180 degrees, keeping a careful listen out for predators.
  8. One of the world’s best-known rabbits is the Warner Bros cartoon character is Bugs Bunny!
